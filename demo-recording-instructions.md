# Demo Recording Instructions

**Goal**: Showcase the "Wow" factor of the 3D mirror and the seamless mood-to-recommendation flow in 60 seconds.

## Setup
1. Open the app in a full-screen browser window.
2. Ensure audio is on (for the Spotify demo, if audible).
3. Clear any previous mood logs (optional, for a clean slate).

## Script & Actions (60 Seconds)

**0:00 - 0:10: Intro & Visuals**
- **Action**: Slowly rotate the 3D mirror with your mouse. Hover over the UI elements to show responsiveness.
- **Voiceover**: "Welcome to MoodMirror. A minimalist smart mirror that reflects not just your image, but your state of mind."

**0:10 - 0:25: Mood Input**
- **Action**: Click the "Tell me how you feel" pill. Select "Self Report".
- **Action**: Type: *"I've had a long week, feeling a bit tired but calm."*
- **Voiceover**: "Simply tell it how you feel, or let the face scan detect your mood instantly."
- **Action**: Click "Analyze".

**0:25 - 0:45: The "Wow" Moment (Transformation)**
- **Action**: Watch the mirror lighting shift to a soft, calming blue/lavender.
- **Action**: The Recommendations card slides up.
- **Voiceover**: "The environment adapts to 'Calm'. It curates my evening: soft textures for the outfit..."
- **Action**: Click the "Outfit" tab/icon. *Wait for the 3D carousel to settle on the 'Cozy' option.*

**0:45 - 0:60: Recommendations & Close**
- **Action**: Scroll down to the Spotify playlist. Click the "Play" button (or hover).
- **Voiceover**: "...a restorative yoga session, and a peaceful piano playlist. MoodMirror isn't just an app; it's your lifestyle sync."
- **Action**: Click the "X" to close the card and return to the spinning mirror.
- **Voiceover**: "Find your balance."

## Tips
- Move the mouse smoothly; avoid jerky movements.
- Let the animations finish before clicking the next item.
- Speak clearly and pause slightly after the "transformation" to let the viewer absorb the visual change.
